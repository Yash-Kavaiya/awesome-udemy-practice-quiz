## 🧭 Recommended Preparation Strategy

1. Review the official certification objectives.
2. Complete the relevant NVIDIA learning resources.
3. Attempt one practice test without external assistance.
4. Review every incorrect or uncertain answer.
5. Study the weak topics identified by your results.
6. Retake the tests until your scores are consistently strong.
7. Revise the complete exam blueprint before scheduling the exam.
